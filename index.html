About Me

Born and bred in Estonia, I have recently moved to France for my Masters studies in Management. Coming from an engineering background, I am able to learn fast, think creative and take advantage of my analytical problem-solving and teamwork skills. What is more, I have thoroughly enjoyed being challenged in a fast-paced and international working environment in my previous work experience and throughout interdisciplinary university projects.

I am also a passionate squash player, having captained for my university’s Ladies’ Squash team and won the Estonian Junior Championships in two consecutive years.
